# Privacy Policy — Widgets

_Last updated: 2026-07-01_

Widgets ("the app") is a home-screen widget customization app. This policy explains what the app
accesses and why. We designed it to keep your data on your device wherever possible.

## What the app accesses

- **Approximate location** (optional): Used only to fetch local weather from Open-Meteo for the
  weather widget. Your coordinates are sent to Open-Meteo (`api.open-meteo.com`) to retrieve
  conditions and are **not stored on our servers**. If you deny location, a default location is used.
- **Notification access** (optional): If you enable it, the app reads the currently playing media
  (track title, artist, playback state) so the music widget can display it. This information stays
  on your device and is never uploaded. You can revoke access at any time in system Settings.
- **Photos you pick** (optional): For the photo widget, an image you choose is referenced by a
  content URI and rendered locally. It is not uploaded.
- **Purchases**: The one-time "Pro" unlock is processed by Google Play Billing. We only store a
  local flag indicating whether Pro is unlocked.

## Data we store remotely

- The app uses Firebase (Firestore/Storage) to deliver an optional catalog of widget designs and
  Crashlytics/Analytics for stability and usage metrics. No personal profile is required to use the
  app. Crash and analytics data are handled per Google's Firebase terms.

## Data we do NOT collect

- We do not sell your data. We do not collect your contacts, messages, precise identity, or browsing
  history. Weather coordinates and media metadata are used only to render widgets and are not
  associated with an account.

## Your controls

- Revoke location or notification access anytime in Android Settings.
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored widget configurations and preferences.

## Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the developer through the app's Play Store listing.
